# Privacy Policy — Nourish Mind and Heart

**Last updated: April 2025**

## Overview

Nourish Mind and Heart ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data Collection

**We collect no personal data.**

The app operates entirely offline. It does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Require an account or login
- Track your location
- Access your camera or photo library
- Connect to any external servers or APIs
- Use analytics or crash reporting services
- Display advertisements
- Share any data with third parties

All content — recipes, meal plans, science data, and shopping guides — is bundled locally within the app and requires no internet connection.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data and it is never transmitted anywhere.

## Children's Privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
